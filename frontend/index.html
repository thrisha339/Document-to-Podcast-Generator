About PodcastAI

An open-source tool that transforms static documents into listenable AI-hosted podcasts.

What is PodcastAI?

PodcastAI is an intelligent document-to-podcast converter that transforms written content into natural, engaging audio conversations. Instead of spending hours reading lengthy documents, you can listen to them anytime, anywhere.

Whether it's reports, study materials, txt files, docx or PDFs, PodcastAI converts your content into a podcast-style discussion between two AI hosts, making information easier to understand and more enjoyable to consume.

How It Works

  • Upload your document.
  • PodcastAI analyzes and extracts the important information.
  • The content is summarized and organized into key points.
  • Two AI hosts discuss the material in a conversational podcast format.
  • The generated podcast is converted into audio and made ready for listening.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make knowledge more accessible by converting static documents into interactive audio experiences, helping users learn, understand, and stay productive wherever they are.
